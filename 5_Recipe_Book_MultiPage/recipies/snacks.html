Vegetable Samosa

About The Dish

Vegetable samosa is a popular Indian deep-fried snack filled with spiced potatoes and peas. It is crispy on the outside and flavorful on the inside, commonly enjoyed with chutney and tea.

List Of Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Green peas
  • Green chili
  • Ginger
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garam masala
  • Oil
  • Salt

Method of Preparation

  1. Prepare dough and potato filling. Shape samosas and deep fry until golden brown.
Nutrition Note: Tasty but high fat

Vegetable Pakora

About The Dish

Vegetable pakoras are deep-fried fritters made using gram flour and assorted vegetables. They are crispy, spicy, and perfect as an evening snack during rainy weather.

List Of Ingredients

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Spinach
  • Green chili
  • Turmeric
  • Red chili powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Method of Preparation

  1. Mix vegetables with besan and spices. Deep fry spoonfuls until crisp.
Nutrition Note: Fried, eat in moderation

Bhel Puri

About The Dish

Bhel puri is a light and tangy street food snack made from puffed rice, vegetables, and chutneys. It is low in oil and full of flavors.

List Of Ingredients

  • Puffed rice
  • Boiled potato
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Sev
  • Salt

Method of Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients well just before serving to keep it crunchy.
Nutrition Note: Light street snack

Vegetable Cutlet

About The Dish

Vegetable cutlet is a shallow-fried snack made with mashed vegetables and spices. It has a crispy outer layer and a soft interior.

List Of Ingredients

  • Boiled potato
  • Carrot
  • Beans
  • Bread crumbs
  • Green chili
  • Garam masala
  • Oil
  • Salt

Method of Preparation

  1. Mix vegetables and spices, shape into patties, and shallow fry till golden.
Nutrition Note: Moderate fried snack

Corn Chaat

About The Dish

Corn chaat is a quick and healthy snack made using boiled sweet corn, vegetables, and spices. It is light, refreshing, and easy to prepare.

List Of Ingredients

  • Sweet corn
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chili
  • Lemon juice
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt
  • Coriander leaves

Method of Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and serve fresh.
