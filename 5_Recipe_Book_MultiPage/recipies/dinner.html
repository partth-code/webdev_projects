  • Curd Rice

    About The Dish

    Besan chilla is a savory pancake made from gram flour. It is high in protein and does not require fermentation, making it quick to prepare. Adding vegetables increases nutrition. It is a popular choice for healthy and fitness-focused diets.

    • Cooked rice
    • Fresh curd

    • Curry leaves
    • Green chili
    • Ginger
    • Oil
    • Salt

    2. prepare tempering
    3. add to rice.

  • Vegetable Daliya

    About The Dish

    Vegetable dalia is a high-fiber, light dinner option. It keeps you full without heaviness and is good for weight management.

    List Of Ingridents

    • Broken wheat (Dalia)
    • Carrot
    • Beans
    • Green peas

    • Cumin seeds
    • Turmeric
    • Oil
    • Salt
    • Water
