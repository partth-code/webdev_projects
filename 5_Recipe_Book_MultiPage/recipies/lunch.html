  • Dal Chawal

    About The Dish

    Dal chawal is one of the most common Indian lunch meals. It is nutritious, comforting, and easy to digest. Rich in protein and carbohydrates, it provides balanced energy and is ideal for daily home meals.

    List Of Ingridents

    • Lentils (toor/moong)
    • Rice
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Turmeric
    • Cumin
    • Garlic

    Method of Prepration

    1. Boil dal with turmeric and salt.
    2. Prepare tadka using cumin, garlic, onion, tomato.
    3. Mix tadka into dal. Serve with steamed rice.
    Complete daily meal

  • Rajma Chawal

    About The Dish

    Rajma chawal is a popular North Indian lunch dish. It is high in protein and fiber, making it filling and satisfying. This dish is perfect for days when you need long-lasting energy.

    List Of Ingridents

    • Rajma (kidney beans)
    • Rice
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Ginger-Garlic
    • Spices

    Method of Prepration

    1. Soak rajma overnight and pressure cook.
    2. Prepare onion-tomato gravy with spices.
    3. Add rajma and simmer. Serve with rice.
    Very high fiber & protein

  • Vegetable Khichdi

    About The Dish

    Vegetable khichdi is a one-pot comfort meal known for being light and soothing. It is often eaten during illness or busy days and is easy on digestion while still being nutritionally complete.

    • Rice
    • Moong dal
    • Mixed vegetables
    • Turmeric
    • Cumin
    • Ghee

    Method of Prepration

    1. Cook rice, dal, vegetables together in pressure cooker.
    2. Add tadka with cumin and ghee.
    Easy digestion, comfort food

  • Roti Sabji

    About The Dish

    Roti sabzi is a simple and everyday Indian lunch combination. It is balanced, affordable, and customizable with seasonal vegetables. This meal supports healthy daily eating habits.

    • Wheat flour (roti)
    • Onion
    • Seasonal vegetables
    • Spices

    Method of Prepration

    1. Make soft rotis.
    2. Add vegetables & spices.
    3. Cook vegetables with onion and spices.
    Nutrition Note: Balanced everyday lunch
