Popular Dishes

Paneer Butter Masala

Indian Delicicy

List of Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter
  • Fresh cream
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt

Method of Prepration

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  5. Add cream, simmer for a few minutes and serve hot.
Nutritional Value: Rich in protein and calcium but high in fats and calories due to butter and cream.

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice batter and filled with spiced potato filling.

List of Ingredients

  • Dosa batter
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onions
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Method of Prepration

  1. Prepare potato masala by sautéing onions and spices.
  2. Heat a tawa and spread dosa batter evenly.
  3. Cook until crispy and golden.
  4. Add potato filling and fold.
  5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
Nutritional Value: Provides good carbohydrates for energy with moderate protein and low fat.

Chole Bhature

Chole bhature is a popular Punjabi dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried bread.

List of Ingredients

  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Chole masala
  • Maida
  • Curd
  • Baking soda
  • Oil

Method of Prepration

  1. Cook chickpeas with onion-tomato masala.
  2. Prepare soft dough for bhature.
  3. Rest the dough for some time.
  4. Roll and deep fry bhature until fluffy.
  5. Serve hot with chole.
Nutritional Value: High in protein and iron but calorie-rich due to deep frying.

Rajma Chawal

Rajma chawal is a comforting North Indian meal made with kidney bean curry served with steamed rice.

List of Ingredients

  • Rajma (kidney beans)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cumin seeds
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Rice
  • Salt

Method of Prepration

  1. Soak and pressure cook rajma until soft.
  2. Sauté cumin seeds and onions.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Add rajma and simmer well.
  5. Serve with hot steamed rice.
Nutritional Value: Rich in plant protein, fiber, and complex carbohydrates.

Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable biryani is a fragrant rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.

List of Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Whole spices

Method of Prepration

  1. Cook rice until partially done.
  2. Sauté onions and vegetables with spices.
  3. Layer rice and vegetable mixture.
  4. Dum cook on low flame.
  5. Serve hot with raita.
Nutritional Value: Provides carbohydrates, fiber, and essential vitamins from vegetables.

Palak Paneer

Palak paneer is a nutritious Indian dish made with paneer cooked in smooth spinach gravy.

List of Ingredients

  • Spinach leaves
  • Paneer cubes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Green chilies
  • Cumin seeds
  • Cream
  • Salt

Method of Prepration

  1. Blanch spinach and make a puree.
  2. Sauté garlic, cumin seeds, and onions.
  3. Add spinach puree and cook well.
  4. Add paneer and cream.
  5. Simmer and serve hot.
Nutritional Value: High in protein, iron, and calcium, making it a healthy dish.
